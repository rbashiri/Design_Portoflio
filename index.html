About Me

I am a Materials Scientist and Data Scientist with over 10 years of experience in scientific research, higher education, and interdisciplinary collaboration. My work focuses on applying data-driven approaches to accelerate discoveries in materials, energy, and sustainability.

With a Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering and extensive experience in materials chemistry, I combine experimental research with Python, SQL, machine learning, and data analytics to transform complex scientific data into meaningful insights and practical solutions.

💡 Fun Fact: I've always been fascinated by understanding why materials behave the way they do. My journey began in the laboratory through experiments and characterization, and today machine learning allows me to explore those same questions from a new perspective— predicting material behavior before stepping into the lab.
